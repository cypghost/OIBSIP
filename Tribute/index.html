Who is Carl Sagan?

Carl Sagan, an American astronomer, astrophysicist, and science communicator, captivated the world with his passion for exploring the cosmos. Born on November 9, 1934, Sagan played a key role in advancing our understanding of the universe and making complex scientific concepts accessible to the public.
